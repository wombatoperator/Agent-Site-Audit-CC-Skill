5. COHORT-WIDE FINDINGS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

   5a. The og:title "Home" Bug (3 domains affected)
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   magnite.com, integralads.com, and openx.com all have og:title set to
   "Home" on their homepage — a WordPress/Yoast CMS misconfiguration where
   the page title override isn't applied in the Open Graph settings. When
   shared socially or summarized by AI, these pages present as "Home"
   with no brand or page context. Fix: set the SEO title in Yoast's
   homepage settings panel.

   5b. Structured Data Coverage — Strong on Schema, Weak on Freshness
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Most WordPress/Yoast sites auto-generate a solid JSON-LD @graph with
   WebPage, WebSite, Organization, and BreadcrumbList. Quality issues:
   • openx.com: dateModified stuck at 2023-06-15 (3 years stale)
   • integralads.com: WebSite description = "The Hidden Cost of MFA
     Webinar" (a campaign title leaked into the brand description field)
   • integralads.com: deprecated plus.google.com sameAs (Google+ shut
     down 2019) still referenced
   • indexexchange.com: Organization logo URL is an empty string
   • advertising.amazon.com: zero JSON-LD — no schema at all
   • doubleverify.com: zero JSON-LD — no schema at all
   • thetradedesk.com: zero JSON-LD — no schema at all

   5c. Navigation Link Quality — Best and Worst in Cohort
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   BEST: openx.com — content cards carry full multi-sentence descriptions
   in link text. Agents understand product value propositions without
   clicking. This is the accessibility pattern every site should adopt.

   WORST: videoamp.com — 37+ undifferentiated generic links ("Read More"
   ×7, "View all Press" ×7, "Read testimonial" ×7, "Read more" ×6).
   Agents cannot distinguish between any content cards in the body.

   5d. The standout: StackAdapt's Demo Form
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   stackadapt.com has the most complete lead-generation form in the entire
   audit cohort — 9 fully labeled fields (First Name, Last Name, Business
   Email, Phone, Company, Job Title, Company Country, Annual Budget, Work
   Type). Every field has an accessible name. This is best-in-class form
   accessibility for agent-driven lead capture.

   5e. The standout: HUMAN Security's Self-Block
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────
   humansecurity.com is blocked by their own BotGuard product — a "Press &
   Hold to confirm you are a human" challenge that headless browsers cannot
   pass. The company that sells bot protection has protected their own site
   from AI agent access. Score: 3/100. This is a notable finding for any
   company considering deploying aggressive bot mitigation.

   5f. The standout: VideoAmp's VideoObject Schema
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
   videoamp.com is the only site in the cohort to include a VideoObject
   JSON-LD block with a complete transcript — a 500+ word script of their
   homepage sizzle video including a Morgan Freeman voice impersonation
   disclaimer. Rich media schema with transcripts significantly improves
   LLM comprehension of multimedia content.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

   QUICK WINS (< 1 week, applicable to most sites in this cohort):

   1. Upgrade Yoast SEO to v26.8+ — auto-generates /llms.txt immediately.
      Applicable to: indexexchange.com, integralads.com, videoamp.com,
      iabtechlab.com, and any other WordPress/Yoast site in the cohort.

   2. Fix og:title "Home" — in Yoast homepage SEO title settings.
      Applicable to: magnite.com, integralads.com, openx.com.

   3. Add Organization JSON-LD schema — at minimum: name, url, logo, sameAs.
      Applicable to: advertising.amazon.com, doubleverify.com,
      thetradedesk.com, stackadapt.com (no Organization schema).

   4. Add aria-labels to social footer icon links.
      Applicable to: virtually every site in the cohort uses icon-only
      social links with no platform name in the accessible label.

   STRATEGIC (1–3 months):

   5. Adopt OpenX's content card labeling pattern — full description text
      in link aria-labels rather than "Read More". This single change
      dramatically improves both accessibility and agent navigation.

   6. Add explicit AI-bot entries to robots.txt — even if the policy is
      "allow all", explicitly naming GPTBot, ClaudeBot, and PerplexityBot
      with Allow: / signals intentional openness to AI indexing.

   7. Add /agents.md (an emerging convention, seen in vercel.com's audit)
      to describe what agents are permitted to do on the site — forms they
      can fill, APIs they can call, content they can summarize.

   leaving its own front doors largely closed to AI agents. Only 3 of 17
   auditable sites (18%) have a functional llms.txt. No site in the cohort
   earned an A grade. The average score of 67.7 puts the industry squarely
   in "functional but agents will struggle" territory.

   a Yoast plugin upgrade, a few schema fixes, and deliberate link labeling
   would move the median score above 80. The companies that make these
   investments first will have a measurable advantage as AI agents become
   primary buyers, researchers, and customers in the advertising ecosystem.
