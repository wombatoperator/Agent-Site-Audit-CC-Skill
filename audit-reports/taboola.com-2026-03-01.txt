8. TOP 3 CRITICAL ISSUES
   ① og:title is "Home" — the fifth site in this cohort with this
     WordPress/Yoast homepage override bug. Social shares and AI content
     summaries receive "Home" as the page title instead of the brand name.
     Fix: set the SEO title in Yoast's homepage settings panel.
   ② 5 generic "Read More"/"Read more" links — agents cannot distinguish
     content card targets. llms.txt helps LLMs find content directly, but
     accessible link labels improve live agent navigation.
   ③ llms.txt covers only Pages — the Yoast auto-generated file appears
     to list Pages only (not Posts, Case Studies, or Thought Leadership).
     Manual curation or a Yoast content type expansion would significantly
     increase LLM content discoverability.
