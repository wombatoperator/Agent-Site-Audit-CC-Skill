   PREREQUISITE  (before any other fix)
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

   • Resolve the domain's identity crisis
     Determine whether www.lotame.com should: (a) redirect permanently
     (301) to a specific canonical destination (e.g., the acquiring
     company's Lotame product page), (b) serve a brand/legacy page
     explaining the acquisition, or (c) be decommissioned with a proper
     410 Gone. The current rotating JavaScript redirect is the worst
     possible outcome — it provides no information, misleads agents and
     users, and scores zero across every audit dimension.

   QUICK WINS  (implement once domain identity is resolved)
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

   • If keeping the domain as a redirect: Use a 301 permanent redirect
     at the server/CDN level (not JavaScript) to a single, stable
     destination page. Include a rel="canonical" pointing to that page.
     Add a robots.txt with a Sitemap pointing to the canonical destination.

   • If serving an acquisition/legacy page: Add a proper robots.txt with
     user-agent rules; add a meta description; add Organization JSON-LD
     referencing both Lotame and the acquiring entity; add OG tags.

   • Write a minimal robots.txt immediately regardless
     Even if the domain is in transition, an empty robots.txt is worse
     than a minimal one. At minimum:
       User-agent: *
       Disallow: /
     ...would at least signal to crawlers that the domain has no
     indexable content, preventing misdirected crawl budget.

   STRATEGIC  (longer-term brand protection)
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

   • Create /llms.txt pointing to Lotame's product documentation
     If the acquiring company maintains Lotame as a named product (e.g.,
     Lotame Spherical, Lotame Panorama ID), a minimal llms.txt at this
     domain pointing to those product pages would preserve AI discoverability
     for searches involving "Lotame data curation" or "Lotame identity."

   • Consider a static archive page
     A single-page HTML document at www.lotame.com describing the
     acquisition, linking to current product documentation, and including
     full structured data (Organization, Product schemas) would score
     dramatically higher and preserve brand equity in AI search.
